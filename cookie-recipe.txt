Ingredients:
 - 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
 - 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
 - 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, room temperature
 - 1/2 cup granulated sugar
 - 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
 - 2 large eggs
 - 2 cups (about 12 ounces) semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips 

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
 2. Whisk together the flour and baking soda
 3. Combine the butter with both sugars.
 4. Add the salt, vanilla, and eggs.
 5. Add flour mixture.
 6. Mix until just combined. Stir in the chocolate chips.
 7. Drop balls of dough on baking sheets lined with parchment paper.
 8. Bake 8 to 10 minutes.
